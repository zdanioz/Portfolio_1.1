02. OFFSET

Offset is not just a coat rack, but a system for the dynamic organization of the wall. The project was created to overcome the static nature of the traditional entryway: a structural rail allows wooden modules to slide and be freely repositioned, creating a visual rhythm that evolves with the user’s needs. It is the balance between the solidity of the infrastructure and the fluidity of everyday use.



TECHNICAL DATA:
Year: 2025
Material: Glass, Aluminum
