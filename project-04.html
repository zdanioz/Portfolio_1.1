04. RAPACI CAPACI

Rapaci Capaci integrates avian ethology into urban maintenance to eliminate scaffolding. Leveraging flight and beak precision, the birds operate in areas inaccessible to humans using a set of ultralight 3D-printed tools. This is not forced training, but a synergy that enhances the animal’s natural abilities, providing an agile, acrobatic solution that respects the species.
